About the Project

BrightSquareProject is an initiative to revolutionize affordable housing by leveraging blockchain technology. Our goal is to fund real estate projects through NFT sales, ensuring community-driven development and sustainability.

With the funds raised, we plan to purchase land in Prince Edward Island and beyond, constructing accessible housing solutions while integrating the power of decentralized finance.

BrightSquare Game

Our NFT holders will receive a unique playable character, a futuristic companion, and BSP tokens usable in BrightSquare Game, a 2D action-packed metaverse adventure.

BrightSquare Token (BSP)

BSP is the core utility token of our ecosystem. It fuels transactions within BrightSquare Game and rewards NFT holders. At the end of 2025, 60% of the remaining BSP tokens will be distributed among NFT holders.
